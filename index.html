Automating Scientific Discovery

As a Research Intern at the University of Maryland, I'm currently spearheading a project to automate insight discovery from scientific literature. My work involves architecting a data pipeline that uses Generative AI and the ArXiv API to extract, analyze, and validate agricultural indices from research papers against real-world satellite data.

This work builds on my foundational research which led to my co-authored publication, "An Overview of Drones in Agriculture."
